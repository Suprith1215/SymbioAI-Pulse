# 🧠 SymbioAI Pulse

### AI Decision Intelligence for Healthcare Insurance

---

## 🚀 Overview

SymbioAI Pulse is an AI-powered system that transforms unstructured medical reports into intelligent, explainable insurance decisions.

It helps hospitals and healthcare providers:

* Predict insurance approval probability
* Detect missing clinical data
* Optimize submissions before approval

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

In healthcare systems:

* Insurance approvals take **2–3 days**
* **20–30% claims are rejected** due to missing data
* Doctors spend time on paperwork instead of patient care

👉 Decision-making happens with **incomplete information**

---

## 💡 Solution

SymbioAI uses a **multi-agent AI pipeline** to:

1. Extract medical data from reports (PDF/text)
2. Match against insurance policy rules
3. Detect missing requirements
4. Predict approval probability
5. Recommend improvements
6. Simulate "what-if" scenarios
